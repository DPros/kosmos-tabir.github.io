Our NGO Kosmos Tabir is now producing obstacles made of metal in Kyiv.
It helps to set up security checkpoints on roads and stop tanks.

Our project name is Space Hedgehog, because the construction that we produce to stop tanks and other military machinery is called їжак in Ukrainian – literally “Hedgehog”

MORE PHOTOS from Space Hedgehog - https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1-RuK7q2Vch6uFq8skhBNUQugOO0CsVgo?usp=sharing

In civil times, we are an Independent Cultural center & Community of Art Makers.
We make cultural, art and social projects.

KOSMOS brings together passionate people, cultural figures, social influencers, artists, cyclists, eco-activists, actors, musicians and people with questions in their heads. We consider art to be a social practice that can unite people from different external worlds and create social interaction between them.

In 2019 we made the first ever art-train in Europe - GogolTrain. More about that - https://ukrainer.net/gogoltrain-en/ . More about our projects and international collaborations here - https://kosmostabir.org/international

Now our office is located on the territory of a shipyard. Since the beginning of the war, we have been collaborating with the local workers and established communication with the defense communities in Kyiv.

We have only been working for a few days, but already have established connections with several random businesses and organizations that provided us with the resources we can use.
Also we are about to start producing bulletproof vests, and already have all the required calculations for that.

Photos of Kosmos Headquarters in the basement of the Shipyard, where 16 people are living now - https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/188VLRAHLt_vJ0tmWKb7qaahcZoOGnnKm?usp=sharing
