NGO "Kosmos Tabir"
...is a Kyiv-based NGO, that unites art communities, creative professionals, environmental and urban initiatives, people from Kyiv, all over Ukraine, European countries and worldwide.

We consider art a social practice that can unite people from different social and cultural backgrounds. This standpoint helped us bring together professionals and amateurs in music, theatre, cinema, audio and visual art, performance and dance, handicraft, digital art, as well as cultural managers and prominent figures, developers of creative products, local small teams and other NGOs in creative sphere. Kosmos Tabir NGO, with it's office in Podil, is the driving force of the neighbourhood, who’s large community is constantly growing due to the numerous art and community building projects and events we realise on a regular basis.


We are interested in establishing partner relations and cooperation/collaboration with other NOG's, creative collectives, communities/teams and artists who share our values, support and develop with modern culture, art, urbanism, street culture, community building, counteraction to gentrification...

OUR PAST PROJECTS
For the past 8 years we’ve managed to independently organize some unique cultural events that involved collaboration of hundreds of people. Some of them are:

  • The first art train in Europe GogolTrain (short film about the project with English subtitles) – in cooperation with GogolFest and the Ministry for Infrastructure, with the financial support from The Embassy of the Netherlands (2019, 2020).
  • Cultural Mobility concept proposal (2019).
  • An international multidisciplinary happening Sanatoria (photos) - a recurring cultural conference with art exhibitions and musical performances on the grounds of governmental sanatoriums (2021).
  • Annual Kosmos Tabir camp-festivals on the riverbank of Dnipro near Kyiv (2014-2021). Next one Kosmos Camp 2022 - link
  • Cultural program, that included experimental music, poetry reading, art market and exhibition within Dream GogolFest in Dnipro (2020).
  • STATION KYRYLIVSKA – This spring we are launching a new project - Station Kyrylivska with partner organizations Podolianochka, Agenty Zmin, Radio Podil, U-Cycle, KyivWhale project, and with the support of members of Kyiv Local Council. Its main goal is to draw attention to the existence of a natural park of Yurkovytsya, and with the help of the community turn this desolated place into a public park.
  • RUBACAVA – An interdisciplinary cultural exchange project, space for artistic interaction between Ukrainian and German artists, organized in collaboration of three teams of Kosmos tabir (Kyiv), Reverberation Fest (Dresden), and Vertuha Arts Festival (Kyiv).
  • Station Karpaty – 3 days rave's in the middle of Carpathian mountains in wild west of Ukraine. Unite people 250 people from, over 20 countries. Project create in collaboration with Berlin creative collective. (link hide under security issue haha called underground baby)
  • NAIBUDEKINO – modern movies event in Cinema House in Kyiv 2 support underground movie community. Project still going on
